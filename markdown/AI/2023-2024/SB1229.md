## SB-1229: Insurance disclosures.

>[!tldr] **SB1229 TL;DR Summary**
> Senate Bill 1229, introduced by Senator Nguyen and effective immediately as an urgency measure, requires property and casualty insurers in California to disclose to applicants and insureds if they use artificial intelligence (AI), including machine learning and generative AI, to make decisions on applications for residential property or automobile insurance, or in claims review and coverage of losses. The disclosure must be provided at the time of application or annually, and these requirements will remain in effect until January 1, 2030. The bill aims to increase consumer awareness about the use of AI in insurance decisions.
