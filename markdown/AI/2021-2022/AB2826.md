## AB-2826: California Platform Accountability and Transparency Act.

>[!tldr] **AB2826 TL;DR Summary**
> Assembly Bill 2826, the California Platform Accountability and Transparency Act (C-PATA), requires large online platforms (25 million+ US users) to provide qualified researchers with access to certain platform data under strict privacy and cybersecurity safeguards set by the Department of Technology. The bill mandates transparency from platforms about content dissemination, algorithm use, and advertising, and requires regular reports to the legislature. It also protects researchers and journalists conducting public interest research from liability if they follow prescribed privacy measures, while imposing civil penalties for violations. The act aims to balance public transparency about platform activities with user privacy and business confidentiality.
