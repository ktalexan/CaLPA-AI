## AB-2412: Healing arts: California Body Contouring Council: practitioners.

>[!tldr] **AB2412 TL;DR Summary**
> California Assembly Bill 2412 (2024), the California Body Contouring Practitioner Act, establishes a regulatory framework for noninvasive, nonmedical body contouring services by creating the California Body Contouring Council. The Council will oversee practitioner certification, premises and mobile permits, and a mandatory 200-hour, physician-approved online training course. Practitioners must meet education, age, and background check requirements, pay fees, and adhere to ethical and safety standards, with annual renewal and continuing education. The Act prohibits the use of medical devices, false advertising of certification, and unauthorized practice, and mandates a public registry, complaint process, and consumer protections to ensure accountability and public safety in this growing industry.
