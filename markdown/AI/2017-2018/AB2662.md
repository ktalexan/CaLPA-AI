## AB-2662: State government: Office of Planning and Research: electronic media: child development.

>[!tldr] **AB2662 TL;DR Summary**
> California Assembly Bill 2662, introduced by Assembly Member Chau, would require the Office of Planning and Research to collaborate with state agencies to establish an independent panel of experts to review and report on the effects of electronic media—including television, video games, social media, mobile devices, and artificial intelligence—on the cognitive, physical, and social-emotional development of children and adolescents. The bill mandates initial pilot projects, the development of a grant program to fund further research, and submission of a comprehensive report to the Legislature by December 31, 2022. Implementation is contingent upon receiving $5 million in private or philanthropic contributions, which would be appropriated for the program between July 2019 and June 2022.
