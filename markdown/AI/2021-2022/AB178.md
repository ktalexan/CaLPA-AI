## AB-178: Budget Act of 2022.

>[!tldr] **AB178 TL;DR Summary**
> Assembly Bill 178, introduced by Assembly Member Ting and enacted on June 29, 2022, amends the California Budget Act of 2022 for the 2022–23 fiscal year. The bill revises, adds, and repeals numerous appropriations and provisions across a wide range of state agencies and programs, including the judiciary, business and economic development, emergency services, health and human services, education, housing, transportation, environmental protection, and more. It provides targeted funding for programs such as wildfire prevention, drought relief, homelessness, COVID-19 response, education (including K-12, higher education, and special education), workforce development, public health, mental health, and social services. The bill also includes allocations for infrastructure, climate resilience, criminal justice reform, and support for vulnerable populations. AB 178 is designed to take effect immediately, ensuring timely distribution and use of state resources in alignment with legislative priorities and federal requirements.
