## AB-1170: Maintenance of the codes.

>[!tldr] **AB1170 TL;DR Summary**
> Assembly Bill 1170, introduced by Assembly Member Dixon for the 2025–2026 California legislative session, is a comprehensive 'maintenance of the codes' bill. It makes nonsubstantive, technical, and clarifying changes across a wide array of California statutes, spanning the Business and Professions Code, Civil Code, Corporations Code, Education Code, Elections Code, Fish and Game Code, Food and Agricultural Code, Government Code, Health and Safety Code, Insurance Code, Labor Code, Military and Veterans Code, Penal Code, Public Contract Code, Public Resources Code, Public Utilities Code, Revenue and Taxation Code, Streets and Highways Code, Unemployment Insurance Code, Vehicle Code, Water Code, Welfare and Institutions Code, and other state laws. The bill implements recommendations from the Legislative Counsel to ensure consistency, update terminology, correct references, and improve clarity without making substantive policy changes. Notable sections include updates related to artificial intelligence, housing, environmental regulation, public health, education, labor, and government operations. The bill does not have fiscal, local program, or appropriation impacts, and is subject to a majority vote.
