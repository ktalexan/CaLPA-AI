## SB-313: Department of Technology: Office of Artificial Intelligence: state agency public interface: use of AI.

>[!tldr] **SB313 TL;DR Summary**
> Senate Bill 313, introduced by Senator Dodd in the 2023-2024 California legislative session, proposes the California AI-ware Act, which would create an Office of Artificial Intelligence within the Department of Technology. The office would oversee and guide the use of AI systems by state agencies to ensure compliance with privacy and civil liberties laws, minimize bias, and promote equitable outcomes. The bill also requires state agencies using generative AI to communicate with the public to clearly disclose when interactions are AI-driven and to provide instructions on how individuals can communicate directly with a human representative.
