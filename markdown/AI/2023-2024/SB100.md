## SB-100: Budget Acts of 2021 and 2022.

>[!tldr] **SB100 TL;DR Summary**
> Senate Bill 100, introduced by Senator Skinner and amended in May 2023, revises the California Budget Acts of 2021 and 2022 by amending and adding appropriations for a wide range of state and local projects. The bill allocates funding across numerous sectors including parks and open space, education, public safety, transportation, housing and homelessness, health and human services, libraries, cultural institutions, and general government. It details specific grants to cities, counties, and organizations for infrastructure improvements, community programs, health initiatives, environmental projects, and economic development. The bill includes provisions for immediate effect as a budget bill, updates administrative and reporting requirements, and ensures flexibility in fund allocation and contract processes. Overall, SB 100 reflects legislative priorities for supporting California's communities and infrastructure through targeted investments.
