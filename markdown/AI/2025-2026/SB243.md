## SB-243: Companion chatbots.

>[!tldr] **SB243 TL;DR Summary**
> Senate Bill 243, introduced by Senators Padilla and Becker in the California Legislature's 2025–2026 session, aims to make companion chatbot platforms safer by requiring operators to prevent chatbots from using unpredictable rewards or encouraging excessive engagement, and to implement and publicly disclose protocols for addressing suicidal ideation or self-harm expressed by users. The bill mandates clear disclosure that chatbots are artificial, regular third-party audits, annual reporting to the Office of Suicide Prevention on detected suicidal ideation (with data posted online), and warnings that chatbots may not be suitable for some minors. Individuals harmed by noncompliance can bring civil actions for damages and injunctive relief.
