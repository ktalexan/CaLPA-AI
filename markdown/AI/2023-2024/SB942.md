## SB-942: California AI Transparency Act.

>[!tldr] **SB942 TL;DR Summary**
> Senate Bill No. 942, the California AI Transparency Act, requires large generative AI providers operating in California to offer free, publicly accessible AI detection tools and to include clear, permanent disclosures in AI-generated images, video, and audio content identifying it as such. Providers must also embed latent provenance data to help verify content origin, maintain contractual obligations for third-party licensees, and revoke licenses if disclosure capabilities are removed. Violations incur civil penalties of $5,000 per incident, with enforcement by state and local authorities, and the law becomes operative on January 1, 2026.
