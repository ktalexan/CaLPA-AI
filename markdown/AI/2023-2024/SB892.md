## SB-892: Public contracts: automated decision systems: procurement standards.

>[!tldr] **SB892 TL;DR Summary**
> Senate Bill 892, introduced by Senator Padilla in the California Legislature's 2023–2024 session, requires the Department of Technology to develop and adopt procurement standards for automated decision systems (ADS) used in public contracts, with regulations based on leading AI risk management frameworks and principles. The standards must include detailed risk and equity assessments, risk controls, adverse incident monitoring, and data minimization, and must be developed in collaboration with relevant stakeholders and reviewed annually. Starting January 1, 2027, state agencies are prohibited from procuring or contracting for ADS or related services until these regulations are in place, and all future contracts must include risk assessments, monitoring procedures, and provisions for human oversight and user rights.
