## AB-2811: Attorneys: court filings: artificial intelligence.

>[!tldr] **AB2811 TL;DR Summary**
> California Assembly Bill 2811, introduced by Assembly Member Lowenthal and amended in March 2024, would require attorneys filing documents in state or federal courts in California to execute and retain for seven years an affidavit certifying whether generative artificial intelligence was used in drafting each document. Upon request by a court, attorneys must file this affidavit, which must indicate either that no generative AI was used or that any AI-generated content was reviewed and approved by a human. The bill aims to enhance transparency and accountability regarding AI use in legal filings.
