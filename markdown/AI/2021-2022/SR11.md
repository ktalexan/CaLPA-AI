## SR-11: Relative to International Women’s Day.

>[!tldr] **SR11 TL;DR Summary**
> California Senate Resolution No. 11, introduced by Senator Leyva and coauthors, designates March 8, 2021, as International Women’s Day, recognizing its historical roots in labor movements and women’s rights activism. The resolution highlights the global significance of the day, the progress made and challenges remaining for women’s equality, and the impact of the COVID-19 pandemic on women, including increased barriers to participation and leadership. It emphasizes the importance of integrating women’s perspectives in all areas, addressing the gender digital divide, and ensuring women’s representation in science, technology, and innovation, including artificial intelligence, to achieve an equal future.
