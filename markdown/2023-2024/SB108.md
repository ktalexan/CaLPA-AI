## SB-108: Budget Act of 2024.

>[!tldr] **SB108 TL;DR Summary**
> Senate Bill 108, signed into law on June 29, 2024, amends the California Budget Act of 2024 by revising, adding, and repealing numerous appropriations and provisions across a wide range of state agencies and programs. The bill implements immediate changes to state government funding for the 2024–25 fiscal year, including operational cost reductions, budgetary loans, and reappropriations. It addresses major funding areas such as education, health and human services, transportation, corrections, housing, environmental protection, and technology. Notable provisions include a statewide 7.95% cut to General Fund state operations, funding for reparations implementation, expanded reporting and oversight requirements, and specific appropriations for artificial intelligence pilot projects, climate initiatives, broadband infrastructure, and higher education. The bill also updates legal definitions, control sections, and indexes for appropriations, and authorizes the immediate effect of its budgetary provisions.
