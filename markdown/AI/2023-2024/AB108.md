## AB-108: Budget Act of 2024.

>[!tldr] **AB108 TL;DR Summary**
> Assembly Bill 108 amends the California Budget Act of 2024 for the 2024–25 fiscal year, making numerous changes to appropriations across a wide range of state departments, agencies, and programs. The bill includes adjustments to funding for the legislature, judiciary, executive agencies, education, health and human services, natural resources, transportation, corrections, and more. It implements cost-saving measures such as a 7.95% reduction in General Fund state operations expenditures, authorizes budgetary loans and fund transfers, and updates appropriations for programs including public health, housing, social services, higher education, infrastructure, and climate initiatives. The bill also introduces new reporting requirements, reappropriates or reverts certain funds, and allows for immediate effect as a budget bill. Notably, it includes provisions for artificial intelligence oversight, reparations funding, and changes to broadband, workforce, and public safety programs.
