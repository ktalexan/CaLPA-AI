## SB-752: The California Master Plan on Tech Equity.

>[!tldr] **SB752 TL;DR Summary**
> Senate Bill 752, introduced by Senator Stern in the California Legislature's 2019–2020 session, establishes the Commission on Tech Equity until November 30, 2024. The commission, composed of appointed experts and ex officio state officials, is tasked with convening public input and researching the impacts of technology—including automation and artificial intelligence—on California's economy, workforce, and public good areas such as privacy, media, democracy, competition, and inequality. The commission will advise state leaders and develop policy recommendations to ensure technological innovation supports an equitable and sustainable economy, with a focus on strengthening the middle class. The commission is advisory only, and its recommendations require further legislative action to be implemented.
