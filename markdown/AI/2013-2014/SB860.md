## SB-860: Education finance: education omnibus trailer bill.

>[!tldr] **SB860 TL;DR Summary**
> SB 860 is California's 2014 education omnibus budget trailer bill, enacting wide-ranging changes and appropriations impacting postsecondary education. The law revises eligibility and award amounts for Cal Grants and the Middle Class Scholarship Program, adjusts funding and reporting requirements for community colleges, and sets new rules for capital expenditures and debt for the University of California and California State University systems. It also mandates student equity plans, expands services for CalWORKs recipients, modifies funding formulas, and provides special provisions for the San Francisco Community College District. Additional provisions include support for neuroscience research via the Cal-BRAIN Act, changes to mandated local program reimbursements, extension of certain fees and reporting deadlines, and updates to fire safety, public contracting, and clean energy funding in higher education. The bill is effective immediately as a budget-related measure.
