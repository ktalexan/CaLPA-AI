## SB-896: Generative Artificial Intelligence Accountability Act.

>[!tldr] **SB896 TL;DR Summary**
> Senate Bill No. 896, the Generative Artificial Intelligence Accountability Act, establishes guidelines for the use of generative AI (GenAI) by California state agencies, emphasizing fairness, transparency, privacy, and accountability. The law requires state agencies to clearly disclose when GenAI is used in communications with the public and provide a way for individuals to contact a human employee. It mandates regular risk analyses of GenAI threats to critical infrastructure, annual reporting to the Legislature, and encourages public-private partnerships for AI workforce development. The Act aims to prevent discrimination, mitigate risks such as bias and disinformation, and ensure GenAI systems are consistent with privacy and civil liberties protections.
