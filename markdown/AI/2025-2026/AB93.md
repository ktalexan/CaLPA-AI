## AB-93: Water resources: demands: data centers.

>[!tldr] **AB93 TL;DR Summary**
> California Assembly Bill 93 (2025) would require owners or operators of data centers to provide estimates of expected water use when applying for initial business licenses and annual water use reports upon renewal, under penalty of perjury. Cities and counties must ensure data centers meet local water efficiency standards, which may vary based on data center size and type. The Department of Water Resources and State Energy Commission must develop guidelines to maximize resource use for technology needs in line with state and federal objectives. Public entities must include data center water usage in demand analyses for setting water service fees. The bill applies statewide, including charter cities, and outlines reimbursement provisions for local agencies.
