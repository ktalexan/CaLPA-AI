## SB-896: Generative Artificial Intelligence Accountability Act.

>[!tldr] **SB896 TL;DR Summary**
> Senate Bill No. 896, known as the Generative Artificial Intelligence Accountability Act, establishes new requirements and guidelines for the use of generative AI (GenAI) by California state agencies. The law directs the Department of Technology and other agencies to update reports on AI developments, mandates annual risk analyses of GenAI threats to critical infrastructure, and requires state agencies to provide clear disclaimers and human contact information when communicating with the public using GenAI. The Act emphasizes transparency, fairness, privacy, and accountability in AI deployment, seeks to prevent discrimination and bias, and encourages workforce development and public-private partnerships to address the evolving challenges and opportunities of GenAI.
