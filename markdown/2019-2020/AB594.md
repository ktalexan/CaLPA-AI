## AB-594: Artificial intelligence.

>[!tldr] **AB594 TL;DR Summary**
> California Assembly Bill 594, introduced by Assembly Member Salas in the 2019–2020 legislative session, authorizes the Director of Technology to designate a position within the Department of Technology to evaluate and advise on the use of artificial intelligence (AI) in state government. The bill requires the department, in consultation with stakeholders and considering recommendations from the Future of Work Commission, to establish guidelines by January 1, 2021, governing the ethical use, efficiency, and integration of AI technologies in state functions, with the aim of benefiting the public and workers.
