## AB-2905: Telecommunications: automatic dialing-announcing devices: artificial voices.

>[!tldr] **AB2905 TL;DR Summary**
> Assembly Bill No. 2905 (Chapter 316), approved on September 20, 2024, amends Section 2874 of the California Public Utilities Code to require that when automatic dialing-announcing devices are used for telephone calls, the initial natural voice announcement must now also inform the recipient if the subsequent prerecorded message uses an artificial voice generated or significantly altered by artificial intelligence. This change, enforced by the Public Utilities Commission, makes violations a crime and creates a state-mandated local program, but does not require state reimbursement to local agencies for compliance costs.
