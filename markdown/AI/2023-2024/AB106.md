## AB-106: Budget Acts of 2022 and 2023.

>[!tldr] **AB106 TL;DR Summary**
> Assembly Bill No. 106, approved on April 15, 2024, amends California's Budget Acts of 2022 and 2023 by updating, adding, and repealing numerous appropriations and provisions across a wide range of state government functions. The bill allocates significant funding for parks and open space, education, public safety, fire prevention, water infrastructure, transportation, housing, homelessness prevention, health and human services, libraries, cultural institutions, and workforce development, among others. It also includes technical amendments, reappropriations, reversions, and administrative adjustments to ensure efficient allocation and use of funds, while granting the Department of Finance authority to make necessary augmentations. The act is designed to take effect immediately to address urgent state budget priorities and projected fiscal deficits.
