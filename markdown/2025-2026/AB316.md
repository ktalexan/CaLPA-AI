## AB-316: Artificial intelligence: defenses.

>[!tldr] **AB316 TL;DR Summary**
> Assembly Bill 316, introduced by Assembly Member Krell in California's 2025–2026 legislative session, proposes adding Section 1714.46 to the Civil Code to clarify liability in civil actions involving artificial intelligence (AI). The bill prohibits defendants who develop, modify, or use AI systems from claiming as a legal defense that harm was caused autonomously by the AI, holding such parties accountable for injuries caused by their AI systems. This measure aims to ensure that responsibility for AI-caused harm cannot be shifted to the technology itself.
