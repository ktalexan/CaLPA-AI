## SB-158: Budget Acts of 2022 and 2023.

>[!tldr] **SB158 TL;DR Summary**
> Senate Bill 158, introduced by Senator Wiener and amended in August 2024, makes immediate amendments to the California Budget Acts of 2022 and 2023, adjusting appropriations and allocations across a wide range of state programs. The bill directs billions of dollars in funding to projects in parks and open space, education, public safety, fire prevention, water and infrastructure, libraries, cultural institutions, transportation, housing and homelessness prevention, health and human services, veterans, community services, general government, and labor. It includes detailed line-item appropriations for hundreds of local and statewide initiatives, including environmental and climate programs, school construction, workforce development, homelessness navigation centers, public health, and digital infrastructure. The bill also revises administrative and reporting requirements for several departments and ensures immediate effect as a Budget Bill.
