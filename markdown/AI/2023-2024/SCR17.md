## SCR-17: Artificial intelligence.

>[!tldr] **SCR17 TL;DR Summary**
> Senate Concurrent Resolution No. 17 (Chapter 135) affirms the California Legislature’s commitment to President Biden’s vision for safe artificial intelligence (AI) and the principles set forth in the 'Blueprint for an AI Bill of Rights.' The resolution acknowledges both the challenges and benefits of AI and automated systems, such as risks to democracy, civil rights, and privacy, as well as opportunities for innovation and efficiency. It specifically commits California to examining and implementing principles like safe and effective systems, protections against algorithmic discrimination, data privacy, transparency, and human alternatives in its legislation and policies governing AI and automated technologies.
