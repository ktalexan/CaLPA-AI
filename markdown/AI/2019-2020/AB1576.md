## AB-1576: Secretary of Government Operations: working group: technology.

>[!tldr] **AB1576 TL;DR Summary**
> Assembly Bill 1576, introduced by Assembly Member Calderon during the 2019–2020 California legislative session, requires the Secretary of Government Operations to appoint a diverse working group on artificial intelligence (AI) by July 1, 2020. This group, representing various sectors and backgrounds, is tasked with evaluating the uses, risks, benefits, and legal implications of AI developed or deployed by California-based businesses. They must recommend best practices to ensure ethical, responsible, and transparent AI innovation that protects consumer rights, addresses bias, and supports workforce development. A report with findings and recommendations is due to the Legislature by July 1, 2021, and the provisions of the bill are set to be repealed on January 1, 2023.
