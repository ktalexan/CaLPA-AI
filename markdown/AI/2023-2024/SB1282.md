## SB-1282: Crimes: diversion.

>[!tldr] **SB1282 TL;DR Summary**
> Senate Bill 1282, introduced by Senator Smallwood-Cuevas during California's 2023–2024 legislative session, amends the Penal Code to expand diversion programs for individuals charged with theft and other offenses. The bill allows county pretrial or health care departments and nonprofit agencies to operate diversion programs, clarifies definitions for theft-related offenses, and requires courts to assess defendants for participation in treatment or service programs. It also broadens judicial discretion to offer diversion to more cases (excluding serious crimes like violent felonies and sex offenses), mandates consideration of treatment and support services, and sets guidelines for program eligibility and completion.
