## ACR-215: 23 Asilomar AI Principles.

>[!tldr] **ACR215 TL;DR Summary**
> Assembly Concurrent Resolution No. 215 (Chapter 206) expresses the California Legislature’s support for the 23 Asilomar AI Principles, developed by experts in 2017 as guidelines for the responsible and ethical development of artificial intelligence. Recognizing AI’s rapid advancements and its potential to impact various sectors, the resolution highlights the importance of ensuring AI aligns with human values, safety, transparency, shared benefit, and long-term societal well-being. The Legislature endorses these principles as foundational values for AI research, policy, and governance, aiming to guide the technology’s growth for the benefit of all humanity.
