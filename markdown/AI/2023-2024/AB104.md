## AB-104: Budget Acts of 2022 and 2023.

>[!tldr] **AB104 TL;DR Summary**
> Assembly Bill 104, introduced by Assembly Member Ting and amended in the California Senate on August 29, 2023, makes extensive amendments to the Budget Acts of 2022 and 2023. The bill appropriates significant funding across a wide range of sectors, including parks and open space, education, public safety, fire prevention, water infrastructure, libraries, cultural institutions, transportation, housing, homelessness prevention, health and human services, veterans' services, community programs, and labor. It details hundreds of specific grants and allocations to state, local, nonprofit, and educational entities for capital projects, program expansions, infrastructure upgrades, and services, with a focus on advancing legislative priorities in environmental protection, public safety, economic development, and social equity. The bill also updates reporting, contracting, and administrative provisions for various state agencies, and ensures immediate enactment as a budget bill.
