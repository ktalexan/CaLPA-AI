## AB-1405: Artificial intelligence: auditors: enrollment.

>[!tldr] **AB1405 TL;DR Summary**
> California Assembly Bill 1405, introduced by Assembly Member Bauer-Kahan and coauthored by Senator Wiener, establishes new regulations for artificial intelligence (AI) auditors. Beginning January 1, 2027, AI auditors must enroll with the Government Operations Agency, pay a fee, and provide detailed information about their qualifications and procedures. The agency will create a public online registry of enrolled auditors and a mechanism for reporting misconduct, with reports retained and shared for enforcement. The bill sets standards for audit practices, requires auditors to avoid conflicts of interest, prohibits employment with auditees within 12 months before or after an audit, and protects whistleblowers. It also creates the AI Auditors’ Enrollment Fund to support these activities.
