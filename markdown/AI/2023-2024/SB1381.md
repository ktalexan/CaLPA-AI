## SB-1381: Crimes: child pornography.

>[!tldr] **SB1381 TL;DR Summary**
> Senate Bill 1381 (Chapter 929), approved in California in 2024, amends sections of the Penal Code related to child pornography to expand existing prohibitions to include materials that are digitally altered or generated using artificial intelligence. The bill criminalizes the production, distribution, possession, and employment of minors in the creation of such AI-generated or digitally altered depictions of minors engaging in sexual conduct, in addition to traditional forms of child sexual exploitation. The legislation also authorizes the forfeiture and destruction of such materials, regardless of conviction, and specifies that no state reimbursement is required for local agencies. The law is contingent on the enactment of AB 1831 by January 1, 2025.
