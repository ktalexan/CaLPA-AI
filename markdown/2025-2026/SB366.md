## SB-366: Employment: artificial intelligence.

>[!tldr] **SB366 TL;DR Summary**
> Senate Bill 366, introduced by Senator Smallwood-Cuevas in the 2025–2026 California legislative session, requires the Department of General Services to contract with the UCLA Labor Center to study the impact of artificial intelligence on worker well-being, job quality, job types, different populations, and state revenues. The department must submit a report of its findings to the Legislature by June 1, 2027, after which the bill's provisions will be repealed. The bill does not require appropriations, involves the fiscal committee, and does not create a local program.
