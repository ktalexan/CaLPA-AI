## AB-107: Budget Act of 2024.

>[!tldr] **AB107 TL;DR Summary**
> Assembly Bill No. 107, known as the Budget Act of 2024, is California's budget bill for the 2024–25 fiscal year, effective immediately upon passage. It appropriates funds for the support of state government, public purposes, and numerous state agencies, including the legislature, judiciary, executive branch, and various departments and programs. The bill details allocations for operations, salaries, capital outlay, local assistance, and special programs, covering areas such as transportation, health and human services, environmental protection, natural resources, public safety, and technology initiatives like artificial intelligence. It includes mechanisms for fund transfers, loans, reappropriations, and reversions, with oversight and reporting requirements. The act also grants authority to the Department of Finance to make technical and fiscal adjustments to ensure legislative intent and operational efficiency.
