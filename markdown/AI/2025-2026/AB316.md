## AB-316: Artificial intelligence: defenses.

>[!tldr] **AB316 TL;DR Summary**
> Assembly Bill No. 316, introduced in the California Legislature by Assembly Member Krell for the 2025–2026 session, proposes adding Section 1714.46 to the Civil Code to address civil liability involving artificial intelligence. The bill prohibits defendants who develop or use AI systems from using the defense that the AI autonomously caused harm in civil actions, ensuring that responsibility cannot be shifted to the AI itself. This measure builds on existing laws regarding liability for negligence and transparency requirements for generative AI developers.
