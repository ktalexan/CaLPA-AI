## SB-893: California Artificial Intelligence Research Hub.

>[!tldr] **SB893 TL;DR Summary**
> Senate Bill 893, introduced by Senator Padilla and coauthors, establishes the California Artificial Intelligence Research Hub within the Government Operations Agency to foster collaboration between government, academia, and the private sector for advancing AI research and development. The hub aims to maximize AI's public benefit while addressing privacy, security, and societal risks by streamlining researcher access to government data (excluding trade secrets and personal information), supporting AI computing infrastructure, promoting innovation, ensuring trustworthy AI development, and expanding educational opportunities. The bill requires interagency cooperation and input from academic institutions, with oversight by the California Privacy Protection Agency on data release.
