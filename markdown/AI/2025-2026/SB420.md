## SB-420: Automated decision systems.

>[!tldr] **SB420 TL;DR Summary**
> Senate Bill 420, introduced by Senator Padilla in the California Legislature, establishes the California AI Transparency Act to regulate the use of high-risk automated decision systems (AI systems) that significantly impact individuals’ rights in areas such as employment, housing, healthcare, education, and more. The bill requires developers and deployers of these systems to conduct impact assessments, implement governance programs to prevent algorithmic discrimination, and provide transparency and appeal rights to affected individuals. State agencies must keep certain assessments confidential, and violations can result in civil penalties and exclusion from state contracts. The bill also exempts small entities and federally regulated systems, and protects trade secrets while ensuring oversight by the Attorney General or Civil Rights Department.
