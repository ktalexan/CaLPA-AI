## SB-1288: Public schools: artificial intelligence working group.

>[!tldr] **SB1288 TL;DR Summary**
> Senate Bill No. 1288 establishes a temporary working group under the California Department of Education to assess and guide the use of artificial intelligence (AI) in public schools. The group, composed of educators, administrators, students, industry experts, and academics, is tasked with developing guidance and a model policy by 2026 for the safe, effective, and equitable use of AI in education, addressing issues such as academic integrity, data privacy, and professional development. The group must report its findings and recommendations to the Legislature by January 1, 2027, after which it will be dissolved; these provisions are set to be repealed on January 1, 2031.
