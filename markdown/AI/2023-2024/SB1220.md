## SB-1220: Public benefits contracts: phone operator jobs.

>[!tldr] **SB1220 TL;DR Summary**
> Senate Bill 1220, introduced by Senator Limón, requires that until July 1, 2030, all California state and specified local agencies providing or contracting for public benefits call center services must use only workers employed in California and prohibits the use of artificial intelligence (AI) or automated decision systems (ADS) to eliminate or automate core job functions of call center workers. Agencies must notify affected workers and the public if AI or ADS will impact core job functions, and contractors must certify compliance or face penalties. The bill removes previous exceptions for health care plans and insurers, extends these rules to local agencies, and allows contract termination for noncompliance, with certain exemptions for emergencies and pre-existing contracts. The legislation declares these provisions a statewide concern, applies them to all cities, and sets procedures for reimbursing local agencies for state-mandated costs.
