## SR-11: Relative to International Women’s Day.

>[!tldr] **SR11 TL;DR Summary**
> The California Senate Resolution No. 11, introduced by Senator Leyva and coauthors, designates March 8, 2021, as International Women’s Day in California. The resolution highlights the historical origins of International Women’s Day, its evolution as a global movement for women’s rights, and the ongoing challenges women face, including those exacerbated by the COVID-19 pandemic such as increased domestic violence and underrepresentation in leadership and technology fields. It emphasizes the importance of integrating women’s perspectives in all aspects of pandemic response and recovery, closing the gender digital divide, and ensuring women’s ideas shape technological advances like artificial intelligence. The resolution calls for reflection, celebration of women’s achievements, and continued advocacy for gender equality.
