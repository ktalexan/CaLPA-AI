## AB-3317: Unfair and deceptive practices.

>[!tldr] **AB3317 TL;DR Summary**
> California Assembly Bill 3317, introduced by Assembly Member Wicks, amends Section 1770 of the Civil Code to expand the definition of unfair and deceptive business practices, particularly regarding online platforms. The bill makes it unlawful for online platform operators to fail to adhere to their publicly presented community guidelines, terms, and content management policies, or to use design, code, or artificial intelligence to manipulate user interfaces in ways that obscure user autonomy, coerce data sharing, or foster compulsive usage. It also requires online platforms to provide a public reporting tool for users to flag banned design practices, with publicly viewable reports, and authorizes the Attorney General to offer compliance guidance.
