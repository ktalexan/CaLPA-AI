## SB-524: Law enforcement agencies: artificial intelligence.

>[!tldr] **SB524 TL;DR Summary**
> Senate Bill 524, introduced by Senator Arreguín in the California Legislature's 2025–2026 session, would require all law enforcement agencies in California to adopt policies mandating that any official report generated fully or partially using artificial intelligence (AI) includes a disclosure statement on each page and identifies the specific AI program used. The bill also requires retention of all AI-generated drafts and an audit trail documenting who used AI, who edited reports, and what video or audio footage was used. These requirements aim to ensure transparency and accountability in the use of AI for law enforcement reporting, and the state would reimburse local agencies for any mandated costs.
