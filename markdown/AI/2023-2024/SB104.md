## SB-104: Budget Acts of 2022 and 2023.

>[!tldr] **SB104 TL;DR Summary**
> Senate Bill No. 104 (Chapter 189), approved September 13, 2023, amends the California Budget Acts of 2022 and 2023, making extensive appropriations and adjustments across a wide range of state government functions for the 2022–23 and 2023–24 fiscal years. The bill allocates billions in funding to support parks and open space, education, public safety, health and human services, water and infrastructure, transportation, housing and homelessness prevention, libraries and cultural institutions, and other community services. It details specific grants and capital outlays for numerous local governments, agencies, and non-profits statewide, with targeted investments in climate resilience, broadband, mental health, workforce development, and equity initiatives. The bill also provides for immediate effect as a Budget Bill and includes numerous technical provisions to ensure timely and flexible disbursement, reporting, and oversight of funds. The act is part of a comprehensive legislative package supporting California's fiscal priorities for the biennium.
