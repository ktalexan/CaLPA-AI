## AB-2876: Pupil instruction: media literacy: artificial intelligence literacy: curriculum frameworks: instructional materials.

>[!tldr] **AB2876 TL;DR Summary**
> Assembly Bill No. 2876 (Chapter 927), approved in September 2024, amends Section 33548 of California's Education Code to require the Instructional Quality Commission to consider incorporating media literacy and artificial intelligence (AI) literacy into curriculum frameworks and instructional material evaluation criteria for English language arts, mathematics, science, and history-social science. Media literacy content must be considered at each grade level and in multiple subject areas during upcoming framework revisions, while AI literacy—defined as understanding how AI works, its applications, limitations, implications, and ethical considerations—must be considered for mathematics, science, and history-social science frameworks and materials adopted after January 1, 2025.
