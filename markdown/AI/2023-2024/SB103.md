## SB-103: Budget Acts of 2021 and 2022.

>[!tldr] **SB103 TL;DR Summary**
> Senate Bill 103, amended in June 2023, makes numerous technical and funding changes to the California Budget Acts of 2021 and 2022. The bill revises appropriations and allocations for a wide array of state programs, including education (K-12, higher education, special education, and preschool), judicial branch operations, energy and climate initiatives, corrections and reentry services, child care, and community programs. It adjusts funding for the University of California and California State University systems, including enrollment targets, student support, infrastructure, and special initiatives. The bill also modifies allocations for local assistance, special grant programs, and capital projects across sectors such as parks, health and human services, public safety, transportation, housing, and workforce development. These amendments are designed to ensure immediate implementation and compliance with legislative priorities, streamline reporting and administrative requirements, and address emergent needs in state and local government operations.
