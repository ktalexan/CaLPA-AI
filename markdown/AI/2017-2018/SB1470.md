## SB-1470: Commission on the Future of Work.

>[!tldr] **SB1470 TL;DR Summary**
> Senate Bill 1470, introduced by Senator Stern in California's 2017–2018 legislative session, establishes the Commission on the Future of Work to study and make recommendations on how technological advancements, especially automation and artificial intelligence, impact workers, employers, and the state economy. The eight-member commission will gather public input, commission research, and advise the Governor and Legislature on policies to ensure technology benefits the workforce and supports a strong, equitable, and sustainable economy. The commission's recommendations are due by November 1, 2019, and it serves in an advisory capacity without direct authority to implement its findings.
