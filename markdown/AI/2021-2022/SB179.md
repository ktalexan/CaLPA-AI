## SB-179: Budget Act of 2022.

>[!tldr] **SB179 TL;DR Summary**
> Senate Bill 179, introduced by Senator Skinner, amends the California Budget Act of 2022, making numerous adjustments, additions, and repeals to appropriations across a wide array of state government functions for the 2022–23 fiscal year. The bill modifies funding allocations and provisions for the judicial branch, local assistance, capital outlay, public health, education, housing, environmental programs, transportation, emergency services, and more. It introduces new grant programs, reporting requirements, and reappropriations, while also updating control sections to reflect legislative priorities and federal funding opportunities. The bill is designed to take effect immediately as a Budget Bill and ensures the state can efficiently manage and adapt its budget to address evolving needs and leverage available federal funds.
