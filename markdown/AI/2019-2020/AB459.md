## AB-459: State government: Artificial Intelligence in State Government Services Commission: report.

>[!tldr] **AB459 TL;DR Summary**
> Assembly Bill 459, introduced in the California Legislature, would require the Artificial Intelligence in State Government Services Commission—if established by the enactment of AB 976—to report to the Legislature by November 1, 2020, on recommended minimum standards for the use of artificial intelligence in state government. These standards must address accountability, human oversight, appeals for AI-driven decisions, transparency, safety, security, privacy protection, and monitoring of AI impacts such as automation, bias, and unforeseen consequences. The bill is contingent on AB 976 becoming law and would be repealed on November 1, 2024.
