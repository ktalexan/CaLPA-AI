## AB-103: Budget Acts of 2021 and 2022.

>[!tldr] **AB103 TL;DR Summary**
> Assembly Bill No. 103 (Chapter 33), approved on June 30, 2023, is a comprehensive budget bill amending the Budget Acts of 2021 and 2022. It revises and adds appropriations across numerous state agencies and programs, including education (K-12, higher education, special education, preschool, and afterschool), health and human services, corrections and rehabilitation, energy and climate initiatives, transportation, public safety, housing, homelessness, parks, libraries, cultural institutions, and workforce development. The bill details funding allocations, reporting requirements, and administrative provisions for a wide array of state priorities, including targeted grants, infrastructure projects, student support services, climate resilience, and community investments. It makes these changes effective immediately to ensure the continued support and operation of California's state government and public services.
