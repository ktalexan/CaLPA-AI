## AB-3317: Unfair and deceptive practices.

>[!tldr] **AB3317 TL;DR Summary**
> California Assembly Bill 3317, introduced by Assembly Member Wicks in 2020, amends the Civil Code to expand definitions and protections under the Consumer Legal Remedies Act, particularly targeting unfair and deceptive practices by online platforms. The bill makes it unlawful for online platforms to violate their own community guidelines, terms, or promises made to users, and prohibits manipulative design or AI-driven user interface changes intended to obscure user autonomy, coerce data sharing, or promote compulsive usage. It requires platforms to provide a public reporting tool for flagging banned design practices and allows the Attorney General to issue compliance guidance.
