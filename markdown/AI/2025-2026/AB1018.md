## AB-1018: Automated decision systems.

>[!tldr] **AB1018 TL;DR Summary**
> California Assembly Bill 1018 (2025), the Automated Decisions Safety Act, aims to regulate the development and deployment of automated decision systems (ADS) that make consequential decisions affecting individuals in areas like employment, housing, education, health care, finance, and more. The bill requires developers and deployers of covered ADS to conduct regular performance evaluations, third-party audits, and provide detailed disclosures to affected individuals, including the right to opt out and appeal decisions. It mandates transparency, accountability, and safeguards against disparate treatment and impacts based on protected characteristics. Enforcement mechanisms include civil actions and penalties, and certain exemptions apply (e.g., cybersecurity, aviation). The bill also amends the Unruh Civil Rights Act and related laws to integrate these new requirements, while protecting trade secrets and proprietary information.
