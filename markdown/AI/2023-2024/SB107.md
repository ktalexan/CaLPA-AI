## SB-107: Budget Act of 2024.

>[!tldr] **SB107 TL;DR Summary**
> Senate Bill 107, as amended in June 2024, is California's Budget Act of 2024, appropriating funds for the 2024-25 fiscal year to support state government operations, public programs, and various state agencies. The bill details allocations for legislative, judicial, executive, educational, health, transportation, environmental, and social services sectors, including reappropriations, reversions, and loan authorizations. It includes funding for infrastructure, education (K-12, higher education, and special education), health and human services (including Medi-Cal, mental health, and public health), public safety, environmental protection, and transportation projects. The bill also provides administrative flexibility for fund transfers and technical adjustments, and sets out reporting and oversight requirements. Notably, it incorporates provisions for the implementation and monitoring of generative artificial intelligence in state government, with monthly reporting and privacy safeguards. The act is effective immediately as the state's main budget bill for the year.
