## AB-979: California Cybersecurity Integration Center: artificial intelligence.

>[!tldr] **AB979 TL;DR Summary**
> Assembly Bill No. 979, introduced by Assembly Member Irwin, amends California law to require the California Cybersecurity Integration Center to develop a California AI Cybersecurity Collaboration Playbook by July 1, 2026, in consultation with relevant state agencies. The Playbook aims to facilitate information sharing across the artificial intelligence community and strengthen collective cyber defenses against emerging threats, drawing on federal standards and industry best practices. The bill mandates both mandatory and voluntary mechanisms for sharing AI-related cybersecurity threat and vulnerability information, ensures the confidentiality of shared cyber threat data, and makes legislative findings to justify limitations on public access to sensitive cybersecurity information to protect state IT systems.
