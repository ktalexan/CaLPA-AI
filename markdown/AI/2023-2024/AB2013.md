## AB-2013: Generative artificial intelligence: training data transparency.

>[!tldr] **AB2013 TL;DR Summary**
> Assembly Bill No. 2013 (Chapter 817), approved on September 28, 2024, adds new requirements to California's Civil Code regarding transparency in the development of generative artificial intelligence (AI) systems. Starting January 1, 2026, developers who make generative AI systems or substantial updates publicly available in California must post detailed documentation on their websites about the datasets used for training, including sources, data types, use of copyrighted or personal information, and whether synthetic data was used. Exceptions are provided for AI systems solely for security, aircraft operation, or national security purposes.
