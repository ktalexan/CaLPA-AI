## AB-3204: Data Digesters Registration Act.

>[!tldr] **AB3204 TL;DR Summary**
> Assembly Bill 3204, the Data Digesters Registration Act, would require California organizations ("data digesters") that design, produce, or substantially modify artificial intelligence (AI) systems or services using the personal data of 1,000 or more individuals or households to register annually with the California Privacy Protection Agency. Registrants must provide detailed information about their AI training practices, including the categories of personal and sensitive data used, whether minors' data is involved, and applicable regulatory coverage. The agency will maintain a public registry, enforce penalties for non-compliance, and administer a dedicated fund from collected fees and fines to support oversight. The bill does not supersede existing privacy laws and becomes operative February 1, 2025.
