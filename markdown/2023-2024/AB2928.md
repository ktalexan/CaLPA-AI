## AB-2928: Budget Act of 2022.

>[!tldr] **AB2928 TL;DR Summary**
> California Assembly Bill No. 2928, introduced by Assembly Member Flora in February 2024, amends the Budget Act of 2022 to revise appropriations for the 2022–23 fiscal year, with immediate effect. The bill allocates funds across a wide range of sectors including parks and open space, education (including artificial intelligence and technology initiatives), public safety, fire prevention, water and infrastructure, libraries, cultural institutions, transportation, housing, homelessness prevention, food access, health and human services, veterans' services, community services, general government, and labor. Notable appropriations support improvements for parks, schools, libraries, community centers, affordable housing, public safety facilities, climate resilience, and workforce development, with funds distributed to numerous cities, counties, and organizations throughout California.
