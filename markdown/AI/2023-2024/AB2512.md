## AB-2512: Telecommunications: automatic dialing-announcing devices: artificial voices.

>[!tldr] **AB2512 TL;DR Summary**
> Assembly Bill 2512, introduced by Jim Patterson and amended in February 2024, updates California's Public Utilities Code to expand the definition of 'automatic dialing-announcing device' to include calls made using an artificial voice, specifically voices generated or simulated by artificial intelligence. The bill gives the Public Utilities Commission authority to regulate these devices and clarifies that violations would be considered crimes, thereby creating a state-mandated local program. However, the bill specifies that no state reimbursement is required for local agencies or school districts due to the nature of the costs involved.
