## SCR-121: International Women’s Day.

>[!tldr] **SCR121 TL;DR Summary**
> Senate Concurrent Resolution No. 121 designates March 8, 2024, as International Women’s Day in California, recognizing the historical and ongoing global struggle for women’s rights, including voting, equal pay, and improved working conditions. The resolution highlights the origins of International Women’s Day in early 20th-century labor movements, its adoption by the United Nations, and the continued challenges women face, such as underrepresentation in leadership and STEM fields, the gender digital divide, and insufficient funding for gender equality. The 2024 theme, 'Invest in women: Accelerate progress,' emphasizes the need for inclusive policies, innovation, and investment to ensure gender equality and a prosperous future.
