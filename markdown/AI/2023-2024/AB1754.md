## AB-1754: Maintenance of the codes.

>[!tldr] **AB1754 TL;DR Summary**
> Assembly Bill No. 1754 (Chapter 131), approved on July 27, 2023, is a comprehensive maintenance bill that makes nonsubstantive, technical, and clarifying changes across a wide range of California codes, including the Business and Professions, Civil, Code of Civil Procedure, Corporations, Education, Elections, Evidence, Family, Government, Health and Safety, Labor, Penal, Probate, Public Contract, Public Resources, Public Utilities, Revenue and Taxation, Streets and Highways, Vehicle, Water, and Welfare and Institutions Codes. The bill implements recommendations from the Legislative Counsel to keep statutory language current, corrects cross-references, updates terminology, and ensures consistency and accuracy in the state's legal framework. These amendments do not introduce substantive policy changes but are essential for the ongoing organization, clarity, and effective operation of California's laws.
