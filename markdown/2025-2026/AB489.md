## AB-489: Health care professions: deceptive terms or letters: artificial intelligence.

>[!tldr] **AB489 TL;DR Summary**
> Assembly Bill No. 489, introduced by Assembly Member Bonta and coauthors, amends California law to prohibit artificial intelligence (AI) and generative AI technologies from using terms, letters, or phrases in advertising or functionality that falsely imply they are licensed health care professionals or that their advice, care, reports, or assessments are provided by such licensed individuals. The bill makes violations enforceable by the relevant health care licensing boards, with each improper use constituting a separate offense, and clarifies that no state reimbursement is required since the bill expands the scope of existing crimes related to deceptive health care representations.
