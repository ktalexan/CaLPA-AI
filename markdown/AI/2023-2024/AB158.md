## AB-158: Budget Acts of 2022 and 2023.

>[!tldr] **AB158 TL;DR Summary**
> Assembly Bill 158 (Chapter 996), approved September 30, 2024, amends the California Budget Acts of 2022 and 2023, making extensive changes to appropriations and allocations across a wide range of sectors, including parks and open space, education, public safety, fire prevention, water and infrastructure, libraries, transportation, housing, health and human services, veterans, community services, and labor. The bill details hundreds of specific grants and funding allocations to cities, counties, nonprofits, and state agencies for capital improvements, public programs, and special initiatives, such as decarbonization, electrification, and workforce development. It also updates administrative provisions, reverts and reappropriates certain funds, and sets appropriations limits and fiscal estimates for the 2023–24 fiscal year. The act is effective immediately as a budget bill.
